How It Works

Step 1: Connect Your Wallet

Start by logging into our platform using your digital wallet. Our site supports major wallets, ensuring a secure and seamless connection. Once logged in, your NFT collection will be displayed in your personal dashboard.

Step 2: Select Your NFT

Browse through your NFTs and choose the one you'd like to see transformed into a unique game element. Click on the NFT to select it and proceed to the next step.

Step 3: Grant Copyright

After selecting your NFT, you'll have the option to grant copyright permission for its use in the game. This process is secure and respects your ownership rights.

Step 4: AI-Generated Customization

Our advanced AI will then generate various styles and appearances for your NFT, ensuring it fits perfectly within the game's aesthetic. You can review and choose the style that appeals to you the most.

Step 5: Integration into the Game

Your NFT is now ready to become a part of the gaming world! It will be transformed into a card or game element in our MTG-style game. You'll get to see how your NFT enhances the gameplay.

Step 6: Play and Earn

With everything set, jump into the game with your new, unique game card. Compete against other players, complete challenges, and earn tokens and rewards. Your journey in the NFT-integrated gaming universe begins!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the NFT Game Integration Platform?

This platform allows you to integrate your NFTs into a digital card game similar to Magic: The Gathering. You can use your NFTs to create unique game cards and play with other members of the community.

How do I start using my NFTs in the game?

Simply connect your digital wallet to our platform. Once logged in, you can select the NFTs you want to use in the game. Our AI will then generate a game-compatible version of your NFT.

Is my NFT safe when I use it on this platform?

Yes, your NFT remains safely stored in your wallet. We only use a digital representation of your NFT in the game.

Can I use any NFT in my collection?

Most NFTs are compatible with our platform. However, there may be exceptions due to technical or copyright reasons. Please refer to our NFT compatibility guide for more information.

Do I earn rewards by playing the game?

Yes! Players can earn in-game tokens and rewards by playing matches, winning tournaments, and participating in community events.

Can I trade or sell the game cards I create?

Currently, cards created on the platform are for gameplay within our ecosystem only. We are exploring options for trading and selling these cards in the future.

How often is the game updated?

What if I encounter issues or have more questions?
